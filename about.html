About Us

From a Small Vision to a Global Solution Partner

ByteGrid Solutions was born from a foundation of innovation and resilience. Founded in 2020 by a group Senior IT Engineers based in the Philippines, the firm quickly became a vital partner for businesses navigating the pandemic through high-quality technical resourcing. By 2023, our reputation for reliability and cross-border expertise allowed us to bridge the gap into New Zealand, providing seamless remote IT solutions that established deep trust within the Australasian market.

In 2025, we underwent a strategic evolution, transitioning from LinkSphere IT into the ByteGrid Solutions of today. Now officially registered in New Zealand and expanding throughout Australia and the Pacific Islands, we have matured into a comprehensive provider of both IT and non-IT solutions. With five years of tested operational experience, we offer a stable, scalable framework designed to empower modern organisations. We don’t just provide resources; we build the partnerships that drive success across borders.

Why Partner With Us?

Proven Legacy: 5+ years of operational excellence and continuous innovation.
Regional Reach: Specialised expertise across the Philippines, NZ, Australia, and the Pacific.
Versatile Solutions: A diversified portfolio spanning traditional IT and strategic non-IT support.
Scalable Framework: Mature, tested processes tailored to meet the demands of a digital world.
